Experience: Owner

Reuben

Written by eye2eye on 14 August 2024.

As the driving force behind his beloved salon Luxe, Reuben leads his team to be the best they can be by leading by example. Reuben’s ‘Can do’ attitude towards hair makes him the natural favourite for clients who want quality, no fuss cuts with a precision edge.

Reuben is constantly pushing for new and exciting services, techniques, products and practices to keep Luxe the salon of choice for fashion forward clients on the North Shore of Sydney. Having travelled the world and by working in London, Miami, Vancouver as well as Italy he brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to the client who visits wanting that “Luxe Experience”.
